About me

As a person

My name is Miika Pihkala.

Autotelic person. An autotelic person needs few material possessions and little entertainment, comfort, power, or fame because so much of what he or she does is already rewarding. Because such persons experience flow in work, in family life, when interacting with people, when eating, even when alone with nothing to do, they depend less on external rewards that keep others motivated to go on with a life of routines. They are more autonomous and independent because they cannot be as easily manipulated with threats or rewards from the outside. At the same time, they are more involved with everything around them because they are fully immersed in the current of life.

Multitalented. Resilient and stubborn. Passion driven, high fluctuations between being lazy and hardworking.

Very open and agreeable personality. Not neurotic. Average conscientiousness. A bit more extrovert than introvert.

I always tend to understand others and not judge them. I don't judge you for having different kind of brains and views than me, it's just how things are in life!

Lack of narcisstic traits other than very high feelings of superiority and self-sufficiency. I actually don't feel like I'm better than most, although I know that I'm better than most in many things. I just think that I've got a lot of good performances from very early on and it has provided me with a great self-confidence. But I also occassionally screw things up miserably and hope to learn something when that happens.

Lack of psychopathic traits.

Social person, but also has hermit tendencies, because of being as much a dreamer as a man can be. So I'm happy with others and on my own, it's a win-win situation! :)

High urge to walk my own road in life and create new and worthy things.

Questions & Answers

What are your favourite game design books?

- My favourite game design book is Designing Games: A Guide to Engineering Experiences [Tynan Sylvester]. My second most favourite game design book is The Art of Game Design: A Book of Lenses[Jesse Schell].

What do you not like that almost everybody likes (in Finland)?

- I don't like to see people I don't know naked or that people I don't know see me naked. I don't enjoy either attention in the internet, or getting popular and followers, but I've understood now how important it is for my gaming career that I get the press attention. It would be useful to have big name in many industries, including games. But I'm very private person in this sense, although very open in most other ways.

Do you have irrational fears?

- Yes, I've got a fear of heights, but it has got better since I visited Sicily, which is really mountainous. So facing the fear has helped me.

Some things that you like?

- Improvisational theatre, standup comedy, movies, games, escape rooms, writing, philosophy, psychology.. ice cream.
